One-time setup

Mashups are made on your own computer by a small helper app — nothing is uploaded, and it uses your machine's speed.

  1. Windows: Download Mashup Studio and double-click it.
    If Windows shows "protected your PC", click More info → Run anyway (we're a small app without a paid certificate).
    Mac: download the helper, unzip, then right-click Start Mashup Studio.command → Open. Use Chrome or Firefox.
  2. The first start installs everything by itself (a few minutes). Then this page reopens on its own, ready to go — you'll just confirm the terms.
